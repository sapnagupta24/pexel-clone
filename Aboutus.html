About us

We are student of School of Programming (SOP) is a transformative initiative dedicated to empowering underprivileged students with high-quality software engineering education. Located in Thakurganj, Kishanganj district, Bihar, this campus is exclusively for girls and operates under a unique self-learning system. This program is jointly managed by Project Potential and Navgurukul, two organizations committed to providing free education to economically disadvantaged students, especially those from rural backgrounds. SOP is one of 10 campuses run across different locations, but the Thakurganj campus is the only one in Bihar. Initially established in Kishanganj, it was later relocated to Thakurganj due to unforeseen circumstances.
